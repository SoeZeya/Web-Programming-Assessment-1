Plot Overview

"The Rookie" is an American crime drama television series created by Alexi Hawley that premiered on ABC on October 16, 2018. The show follows the story of John Nolan (played by Nathan Fillion), a man in his forties who decides to pursue his dream of becoming a Los Angeles Police Department officer. Despite facing skepticism from his colleagues and family due to his age, Nolan perseveres and navigates the challenges of being a rookie cop in one of the most dangerous cities in the United States. Throughout the series, viewers follow Nolan as he goes through the trials and tribulations of police training, building relationships with his fellow officers, and tackling various cases ranging from routine traffic stops to high-stakes criminal investigations. The show also delves into the personal lives of the characters, exploring their struggles, triumphs, and the impact of their profession on their families and personal relationships. "The Rookie" has been praised for its engaging storyline, well-developed characters, and the charismatic performance of Nathan Fillion in the lead role. It blends elements of drama, action, and humor while offering insights into the complexities of law enforcement and the human experience. The show has garnered a dedicated fanbase and has been renewed for multiple seasons, continuing to entertain audiences with its thrilling police procedural format.
