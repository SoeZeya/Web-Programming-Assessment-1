Spicy Peanut Noodles

A quick and easy weeknight meal packed with flavor and perfect for a light lunch. This dish is customizable with your favorite vegetables and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound noodles (e.g., soba noodles, rice noodles, spaghetti)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter (creamy or chunky)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha (or other chili sauce, adjust to preference)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Optional: Vegetables (e.g., shredded carrots, chopped bell peppers, sliced cucumbers)
  • Optional: Protein (e.g., grilled chicken, tofu, shrimp)

Method

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, sriracha, honey, rice vinegar, lime juice, garlic, and ginger until smooth.
  3. Toss the noodles with the peanut sauce until well coated.
  4. Add your desired vegetables and protein (if using) and toss to combine.
  5. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve immediately.
